Essential functions of cookies on our platform:

  • Maintaining your secure login session throughout your visit
  • Remembering your language, currency (GBP), and location preferences
  • Storing your game preferences and bonus eligibility status
  • Ensuring the security of your account and financial transactions

  • Loading pages faster by storing frequently accessed information

Without certain cookies, essential features of Monkey Mobile would not function properly. For example, we wouldn't be able to keep you logged in as you move between games or maintain the security of your financial transactions. We carefully balance cookie usage to enhance your experience while respecting your privacy choices.
